Info About Me

I am a 20 years old individual currently studying Diploma in New Media Communication and Contentpreneurship.  My main cores is digital content creation, web development,  animation and game creation.  I am very passionate about game designing and becoming a  tester for a game. I first started delving into the game community when i was 10 years old. My first game that i had played is Farmville in Facebook. Since then, i am curious and fascinated with the game development and how it came to be. In 2013, i have had an experience creating a game with my brother in Roblox Studio and Byond. I used Roblox's script tool to insert and extract materials to put in a game while in Byond, i drag and down the materials needed to create a game. My hobbies are playing games and sketch draws. In my free time, i would sketch characters and objects.         

Expertise and Experience

During my studies, i have learned on how to create game using Unreal Engine. Following the guidance of my lecturers and Youtube tutorials, i am capable of making interactions and nodes within the game. I also intermediate in applying textures in materials and world map environment. Besides that, i am knowledgeable of how Unity operates and coding. I know how to do basic coding and understand the functions of the codes. Me and my brother learned and experienced creating a 2D pixel game in Byond using drag and drop tool with a simple coding functions. I also haved the experienced using Roblox Studio and the script tool maker to create an interactive game in Roblox. Other than that, i dabble in videography and animations for my group assignments which is Adobe Animate and Adobe Premier Pro. I can use those software intermediate with the understanding of each tools and functions. I have created Animation styles using GIF for my assignment.

Academic History

I went to SK Seksyen 7 for about two month when i was 7 years old and moved overseas following my mother doing her PhD in England, Preston city. I was schooled near to where my house is which is Deepdale Community Primary School for 4 and a half years and then moved back to Malaysia because my mother had finished her PhD. Afterwards, i was schooled back to my old school which is SK Seksyen 7 to finish my primary school and take UPSR. Following my results, i continue to go to my high school, SMK Jalan Reko, Kajang. After SPM, i applied for UiTM Lendu, Alor Gajah for my continuation studies which is Diploma in New Media Communication and Contentpreneurship.
